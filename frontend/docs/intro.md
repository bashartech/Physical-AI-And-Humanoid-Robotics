## üåç What Is Physical AI?

**Physical AI** is the science of building artificial intelligence that interacts with the real world through bodies, sensors, motors, and physical constraints.

Where classical AI lives inside computers, *Physical AI lives in space*.

It understands:
- gravity  
- friction  
- balance  
- force  
- motion  
- human environments  
- and the messy, unpredictable reality we live in  

Humanoid robots ‚Äî robots shaped like us ‚Äî represent the highest expression of Physical AI. Their body form allows them to use tools, open doors, climb stairs, walk over uneven terrain, and operate in human-designed spaces.

This book teaches you how to build the systems powering those robots.
