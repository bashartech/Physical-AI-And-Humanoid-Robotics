# Chapter 1: What VLA Represents

## Introduction
This chapter introduces Vision-Language-Action (VLA) systems, highlighting their transformative potential in robotics and AI. It explores how integrating vision, language, and action capabilities allows robots to understand and interact with the world in more intuitive, human-like ways. We will set the stage for understanding the foundational concepts and the exciting future VLA promises.\n
