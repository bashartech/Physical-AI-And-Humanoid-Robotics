# Chapter 6: Manipulation

### Introduction
This chapter focuses on robot manipulation, the ability of robots to interact physically with objects in their environment. The goal is to intuitively explain the mechanics, control, and intelligence required for robots to grasp, move, and assemble objects, transforming abstract tasks into physical actions.\n
